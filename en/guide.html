songGate guide

data required for each release

  1. album/release titles
  2. language and genre
  3. cover art (3000x3000 pixels)
  4. audio files (must be .wav, if they aren't we will help you convert)
  5. people involved and roles (singer, producer, songwriter, etc) [first name + last name, we can use your artist name as first name and give you a suggested last name if needed]
  6. lyrics (if any)
  7. preview start time (optional)
  8. youtube content ID yes/no (whether or not to collect revenue from all videos on youtube using your music)
  9. release date (give timezone, can be accurate to the minute)

note that lyrics will take some time (max 2 weeks) to go live after the release

data reporting

  1. streaming data will be sent on the 2nd of every month, UTC+8
  2. balance, total interest earned, and cycles paid will also be reported

revenue collection

  1. clients can request a withdrawal by email at any time
  2. the full balance will be sent through a payment processer or bank transfer, typically within 24 hours. amounts above S$10k might take a longer time, up to 5 days
